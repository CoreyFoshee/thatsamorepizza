Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about That's Amore Pizzeria

Do you deliver?

Yes! We offer delivery through our online ordering system and major delivery platforms. Delivery radius and fees may apply.

Can I place a large order for pickup?

Absolutely! We recommend calling ahead for large orders (10+ pizzas) to ensure timely preparation. You can also order online.

Do you offer gluten-free options?

We currently offer gluten-free pizza crusts. Please let us know about any dietary restrictions when ordering.

What's the difference between your pizza styles?

Our New York style features thin, crispy crust that's foldable, while our Chicago style has a thick, buttery crust with high sides and layers of toppings.

Do you cater events?

Yes! We offer comprehensive catering services for corporate events, parties, and special occasions. Contact us for a custom quote.
